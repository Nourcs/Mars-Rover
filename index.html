Mars Rover

To move your a rover :

  • Open the console of your navigator by clickin F12.
  • Type in : commands("", rovers[1]) to see the map! ( Only if you uncomment line 226 in javascripts/app.js)
  • Then to move your rover, choose between rovers[1] and rovers[2].
    Then add the instructions "l" to turn left, "r" to turn right, "f" to move forward and "b" to move backwards.
    A good example to begin with is : commands("rffff",rovers[2])
